# Issuer FAQ: CURATE

### 1. What happens if my oracle node gets slashed?
If your oracle node is flagged for feeding false data:
- Your CURATE Passport SFT is burned.
- Your 75 HAC collateral is sent to the Hacash Penalty Pool and locked for 120 days.
- Any active CRT stakes associated with your node are forfeited to the dispute judges.

### 2. How do I de-bond my oracle?
You can safely de-bond your oracle at any time if there are no active disputes. You initiate a de-bonding transaction which triggers a 14-day cooling freeze. Once the freeze ends, your 75 HAC + 1 HACD are returned to your wallet.

### 3. What is the utility of the CRT token?
CRT is the fungible utility token used to pay for queries to bonded oracles, stake on node reputational indexes, and participate in governance voting. All CRT tokens originate from stack-formation events (0% pre-mine).
