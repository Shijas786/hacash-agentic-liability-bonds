# HacashBuilders on HACD

## Hero

**Builder identity, formed through HACD.**

HacashBuilders issues BUILD — a hybrid community asset where each HACD becomes a permanent Builder ID and forms a fixed share of a hard-capped supply of 10,000,000 units.

## What is HacashBuilders?

HacashBuilders is a builder-community asset issued through the HACD Stack protocol. Unlike typical community tokens deployed at zero cost, every BUILD unit is formed through a real HACD container and a real HAC formation cost — and the unique HACD name behind each lot becomes a permanent Builder ID.

There are exactly 500 Stack lots. Each lot uses 1 HACD and 50 HAC to form 20,000 BUILD plus one Builder ID. Once all 500 lots are filled, no more BUILD and no more Builder IDs can ever be created.

## Why HACD?

Most community tokens are issued by running a contract and pressing a button. Anyone can create 10,000,000 tokens in seconds for almost nothing, and "membership" is just a row in a database.

HacashBuilders is different.

- Each BUILD unit exists because a PoW-mined HACD was committed as its container.
- Each lot's unique HACD name becomes a verifiable Builder ID — a scarce identity, not a database entry.
- Each lot required 50 HAC as an on-chain formation cost.
- The formation history is permanent and visible on the Hacash explorer.
- Supply cannot increase because no additional HACD lots are available after launch.

Bitcoin proved PoW for money. HACD brings PoW to assets.

## The hybrid: BUILD + Builder ID

One Stack action gives you two linked things:

- **BUILD** — a fungible balance. 20,000 BUILD per lot. All BUILD is interchangeable.
- **Builder ID** — your unique 6-letter HACD name, registered on-chain as your builder identity for that lot.

You do not transact twice. A single Stack forms both.

## How Stack works

1. You bring 1–10 HACD units. Their names will become your Builder IDs.
2. You bring enough HAC: (number of HACD) × 50 HAC + a small network fee.
3. You confirm a Stack transaction on the Launchpad (up to 200 HACD names per transaction).
4. Your HACD containers form BUILD and register your Builder ID(s). You receive 20,000 BUILD per lot.
5. Your formation is recorded on-chain permanently.

Your HACD is not consumed — it is used as the formation container. If you later remove the Stack, the HACD is released but the BUILD for that lot is burned and the Builder ID is retired.

## Formation rules

- Total HACD lots: 500
- Units per lot: 20,000 BUILD
- Builder IDs: up to 500 (one unique HACD name each)
- Stack cost: 50 HAC per HACD
- Total supply cap: 10,000,000 BUILD (hard cap, no exceptions)
- Minimum per participant: 1 HACD
- Maximum per participant: 10 HACD
- Phase model: public (no allowlist, no reserved allocation)
- All lots are equal — no tiers

## FAQ

**Do I need HACD to participate?**
Yes. Each Stack lot requires 1 HACD. You must hold HACD before you can form BUILD.

**Do I need HAC?**
Yes. Each HACD lot requires 50 HAC as the formation cost plus a small network fee.

**How much BUILD do I get per HACD?**
20,000 BUILD per HACD lot, plus one Builder ID.

**Can I participate with more than 10 HACD?**
No. The maximum per participant is 10 HACD lots.

**What happens if I unstake?**
Removing a Stack releases your HACD but permanently burns the 20,000 BUILD tied to that lot and retires the Builder ID. The HAC formation cost is not refunded.

**Is BUILD an investment?**
No. BUILD is a community membership Stack Asset. No price, return, or liquidity is guaranteed.
