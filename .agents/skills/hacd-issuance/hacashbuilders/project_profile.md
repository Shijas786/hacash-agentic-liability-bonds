# Project Profile: HacashBuilders

## One-liner

HacashBuilders is a builder-community asset formed through HACD Stack, where each unique HACD becomes a permanent Builder ID and forms a share of the fungible BUILD token.

## Category

Community (hybrid FT + NFT)

## Problem

Builder communities usually issue membership through free, contract-deployed tokens with no formation cost and no scarce, verifiable identity behind each member. Anyone can mint a million tokens at zero cost, and "membership" is just a number in a database. HacashBuilders uses HACD to make builder membership costly to form, permanently capped, and tied to a unique on-chain identity.

## Asset concept

HacashBuilders is a hybrid asset issued across 500 HACD lots.

- **FT component (BUILD)**: Each lot forms exactly 20,000 BUILD units. Total supply is fixed at 10,000,000 BUILD. Supply cannot increase because no additional HACD lots exist beyond the 500 defined at launch.
- **NFT component (Builder ID)**: Each lot's unique 6-letter HACD name acts as a permanent Builder ID — a distinct on-chain identity for that member. Up to 500 Builder IDs can exist.

Removing a Stack releases the HACD (and its Builder ID returns to the holder's free HACD) but burns the 20,000 BUILD tied to that lot, permanently reducing supply.

## Why HACD

- HACD provides a PoW container that cannot be faked or cheaply replicated.
- The unique HACD name gives each builder a scarce, verifiable identity — the NFT half of the hybrid that a plain FT contract cannot provide.
- Stack cost (50 HAC per HACD) creates a visible cost basis reference.
- Formation history is on-chain and permanent, giving HacashBuilders a credible origin story.
- Hard-capped lot count (500) prevents supply inflation after launch.

## Target users

- Hacash builders who want a meaningful, identity-backed community membership.
- HACD collectors who want to put idle HACD units to productive use as Builder IDs.
- Contributors who want transparent, verifiable membership records rather than database entries.
