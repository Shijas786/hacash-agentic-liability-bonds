# Incubator Fit Review: HacashBuilders

## Verdict

Strong fit

## Why it fits HACD

- Supply is hard-capped by the number of available HACD lots (500), not by a contract parameter anyone can change later.
- The builder-membership framing maps naturally to HACD's per-unit identity model: each unique HACD name becomes one permanent Builder ID.
- A hybrid FT+NFT structure uses HACD for exactly what it is good at — the FT component (BUILD) carries fungible community weight while the NFT component (the HACD name) carries a unique, non-transferable-by-design formation identity.
- Formation cost in HAC creates a real, on-chain cost basis reference that distinguishes BUILD from a costless airdrop or free mint.
- Removing a Stack lot burns the corresponding BUILD units, tying asset supply permanently to active HACD containers.

## What HACD adds

- **PoW container**: Each of the 500 HACD lots is a PoW-mined container. Builder identities are not artificially created — they are tied to real mined, bid-for assets.
- **Per-lot identity (NFT side)**: The unique 6-letter HACD name is the Builder ID. 500 lots = up to 500 distinct on-chain builder identities, each independently verifiable.
- **Formation cost**: 50 HAC per HACD lot. Total formation cost reference is 25,000 HAC. This makes participation visible and non-trivial.
- **Asset history**: Every formation event is inscribed on-chain. The community can see exactly which HACD lots became builders and when.
- **Distributed formation**: The 1–10 HACD per participant cap prevents any single participant from absorbing the full 500-lot cohort.

## Main concerns

- The hybrid FT+NFT framing is more complex than a pure FT. Copy must clearly separate the fungible BUILD balance from the unique HACD Builder ID so participants are not confused about what is and is not transferable.
- Future utility (governance weight, builder-network access, reputation features) depends on development not yet complete. Copy must not promise these as certain.
- 500 lots is 5× the StackFire reference (100). HACD Labs should confirm there is enough HACD supply and builder demand to absorb 500 lots in a single public phase without stalling.
- Issuer identity / team accountability should be confirmed to HACD Labs privately before approval.

## Required issuer confirmations

- Confirm 50 HAC per HACD is the final stack cost, not a placeholder.
- Confirm 20,000 BUILD per HACD lot (→ 10,000,000 total) is final.
- Confirm the maximum of 10 HACD per participant is enforced at the Launchpad level.
- Confirm the hybrid model: HACD name = Builder ID (NFT), BUILD = fungible balance (FT). Confirm which (if either) is intended to be transferable.
- Confirm governance, reputation, and builder-network access are marked as planned, not promised.
- Confirm the team / operator identity to HACD Labs privately before approval.
