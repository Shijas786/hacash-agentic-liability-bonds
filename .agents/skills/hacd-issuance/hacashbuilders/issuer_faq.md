# HacashBuilders (BUILD) — Issuer FAQ

## Do I need HACD to participate?

Yes. Each Stack participation requires 1 HACD per lot according to the HacashBuilders launch rules. Without HACD you cannot form BUILD or register a Builder ID.

## Do I need HAC?

Yes. HAC is required for two things: the stack cost (50 HAC per HACD lot) and the Hacash network transaction fee. Make sure you have enough HAC for both before you begin.

## How much HAC do I need for 1 lot?

50 HAC for the stack cost plus a small network fee. The network fee varies and should be checked on explorer.hacash.org at the time of your transaction.

## How many lots can I take?

Between 1 and 10 HACD lots per participant.

## How many BUILD will I receive?

20,000 BUILD per HACD lot, plus one Builder ID per lot. If you Stack 3 HACD lots you will receive 60,000 BUILD and 3 Builder IDs.

## What is a Builder ID?

Your Builder ID is the unique 6-letter HACD name you used for that lot, registered on-chain. BUILD is fungible; the Builder ID is unique to you and identifies which builder formed that lot. Both come from the same single Stack action.

## Is there a reserved allocation for the team?

No. There are no reserved lots, no team allocation, and no designated address requirement. All 500 lots are available through the public phase on a first-come basis.

## What happens when all 500 lots are filled?

No more BUILD can ever be formed and no more Builder IDs can be created. Total supply is permanently capped at 10,000,000 BUILD. The Launchpad will show the issuance as completed.

## What happens if I remove my Stack?

Removing the Stack releases your HACD unit. However, the 20,000 BUILD tied to that lot are permanently burned and the Builder ID is retired. Your HAC formation cost is not refunded. This is by design — it links BUILD supply directly to active HACD containers.

## Is BUILD listed on any exchange?

No listing is guaranteed. HacashBuilders has not arranged any exchange listing. Do not assume BUILD will be tradable on any platform after launch.

## Is this an investment product?

No. BUILD is a HACD Stack Asset for builder-community membership. No profit, yield, price floor, or investment return is guaranteed or implied.

## When will governance and builder-network features be available?

These are planned features that depend on future development. They are not available at launch and are not guaranteed. Follow official HacashBuilders channels for updates.

## Where can I verify my formation?

All Stack formations are recorded on the Hacash blockchain. You can verify your lot, BUILD balance, and Builder ID on the Hacash explorer (explorer.hacash.org) or the HACD Launchpad after your transaction is confirmed.
