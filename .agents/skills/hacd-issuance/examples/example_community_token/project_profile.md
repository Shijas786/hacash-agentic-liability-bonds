# Project Profile: StackFire

## One-liner

StackFire is a community membership asset formed through HACD Stack, giving each holder a verifiable PoW-backed formation record.

## Category

Community

## Problem

Most community tokens are deployed through a contract with no real formation cost and no visible on-chain history tied to a scarce asset. Anyone can create a million tokens at zero cost. StackFire uses HACD to make community membership formation visible, costly, and traceable.

## Asset concept

SFR is a fungible token (FT) issued across 100 HACD lots. Each lot forms exactly 10,000 SFR units. Total supply is fixed at 1,000,000 SFR. Supply cannot increase because no additional HACD lots are available beyond the 100 defined at launch. Removing a stack burns the SFR tied to that lot, permanently reducing supply.

## Why HACD

- HACD provides a PoW container that cannot be faked or cheaply replicated.
- Stack cost (50 HAC per HACD) creates a visible cost basis reference that a plain token contract cannot provide.
- Formation history is on-chain and permanent, giving StackFire a credible origin story.
- Hard-capped lot count prevents supply inflation after launch.

## Target users

- Hacash builders who want a meaningful community membership token.
- HACD collectors who want to put their HACD units to productive use.
- Community contributors who want transparent, verifiable membership records.
