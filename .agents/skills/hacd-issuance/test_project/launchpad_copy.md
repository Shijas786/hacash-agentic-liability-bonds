# StackFire on HACD

## Hero

**Community membership, formed through HACD.**

StackFire issues SFR — a community Stack Asset with a visible PoW formation cost and hard-capped supply of 1,000,000 units.

## What is StackFire?

StackFire is a community membership token issued through the HACD Stack protocol. Unlike typical community tokens that are deployed at zero cost, every SFR unit is formed through a real HACD container and a real HAC formation cost. The result is a community asset with a traceable, permanent on-chain origin.

There are exactly 100 Stack lots. Each lot uses 1 HACD and 50 HAC to form 10,000 SFR. Once all 100 lots are filled, no more SFR can ever be created.

## Why HACD?

Most community tokens are issued by running a contract and pressing a button. Anyone can create 1,000,000 tokens in seconds for almost nothing.

StackFire is different.

- Each SFR unit exists because a PoW-mined HACD was committed as its container.
- Each lot required 50 HAC as an on-chain formation cost.
- The formation history is permanent and visible on the Hacash explorer.
- Supply cannot increase because no additional HACD lots are available after launch.

Bitcoin proved PoW for money. HACD brings PoW to assets.

## How Stack works

1. You bring 1–10 HACD units.
2. You bring enough HAC: (number of HACD) × 50 HAC + a small network fee.
3. You confirm a Stack transaction on the Launchpad.
4. Your HACD containers are used to form SFR. You receive 10,000 SFR per HACD lot.
5. Your formation is recorded on-chain permanently.

Your HACD is not consumed — it is used as the formation container. If you later remove the Stack, the HACD is released but your SFR units for that lot are burned.

## Formation rules

- Total HACD lots: 100
- Units per lot: 10,000 SFR
- Stack cost: 50 HAC per HACD
- Total supply cap: 1,000,000 SFR (hard cap, no exceptions)
- Minimum per participant: 1 HACD
- Maximum per participant: 10 HACD
- Phase model: public (no allowlist, no reserved allocation)
- All lots are equal — no tiers

## FAQ

**Do I need HACD to participate?**
Yes. Each Stack lot requires 1 HACD. You must hold HACD before you can form SFR.

**Do I need HAC?**
Yes. Each HACD lot requires 50 HAC as the formation cost plus a small network fee.

**How many SFR do I get per HACD?**
10,000 SFR per HACD lot.

**Can I participate with more than 10 HACD?**
No. The maximum per participant is 10 HACD lots.

**What happens if I unstake?**
Removing a Stack releases your HACD but permanently burns the 10,000 SFR tied to that lot. The HAC formation cost is not refunded.

**Is SFR an investment?**
No. SFR is a community membership Stack Asset. No price, return, or liquidity is guaranteed.
