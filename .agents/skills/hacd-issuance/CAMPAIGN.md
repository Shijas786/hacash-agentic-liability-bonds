# HACD Stack Token Incubator — Cohort 2 Campaign

## What This Is

HACD Labs is running Cohort 2 of the HACD Stack Token Incubator. We are looking for 10 quality projects to launch real Stack Assets on the HACD Launchpad. Selected projects will be supported through the full issuance process using the HACD Incubator AI Issuance Skill.

Apply here: https://hacd.it/incubator
Launchpad: https://hacd.it/launchpad

## Who Should Apply

- Builders with a real project idea that benefits from PoW-backed asset formation
- Community operators who want credible, on-chain membership assets
- AI agent builders exploring HACD as an identity container
- Artists and creators exploring hybrid FT/NFT/SFT structures
- Anyone who wants to issue something meaningful on Hacash — not just deploy a token

You do not need to be a developer. The AI skill handles the technical structure. You need a real idea and the commitment to see it through.

## How It Works

1. Fill the application form at https://hacd.it/incubator (10-15 minutes)
2. HACD Labs reviews all applications and selects up to 30 projects
3. Selected builders receive the AI skill and quickstart guide
4. Each builder generates their full 8-document launch package using Claude or ChatGPT
5. Builders run the validator and submit their package to HACD Labs
6. HACD Labs reviews submissions and selects the top 10
7. Top 10 projects launch on hacd.it/launchpad
8. Reward pool distributed based on final ranking

## Judging Criteria

Each submission is scored across 5 dimensions using the project scorer prompt:

- **PoW fit** — does this project genuinely need HACD or could it be a plain token?
- **Supply logic** — is the math clean, consistent, and verifiable?
- **Utility clarity** — is utility at launch honest and clearly separated from roadmap?
- **Copy safety** — does all public copy avoid unsafe promises, price language, and legal risk?
- **Team credibility** — is the team identifiable and committed to launching?

## What a Valid Submission Includes

Every submission must contain all of the following:

1. `issuer_intake_form.md` — completed with real project details
2. `incubator_fit_review.md` — generated by the AI skill
3. `project_profile.md` — generated by the AI skill
4. `stack_design.md` — generated by the AI skill
5. `launch_spec.json` — generated by the AI skill, must pass validator with no ERRORs
6. `launchpad_copy.md` — generated by the AI skill
7. `issuer_faq.md` — generated by the AI skill
8. `x_announcement.md` — generated by the AI skill
9. `review_checklist.md` — generated by the AI skill
10. Validator output screenshot or paste showing `OK: launch spec passed basic validation`

Submit as a ZIP file or GitHub repo link.
